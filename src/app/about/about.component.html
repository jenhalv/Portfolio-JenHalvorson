About Me Greetings! Thanks for checking out my portfolio.  My name is Jennifer Halvorson & I ventured into the programming world in 2019 after quitting my teaching job in the KC area to move to our family’s southwestern Missouri pecan farm.

I created this portfolio using Angular CLI and published Paul’s Pecans, a website dedicated to displaying information about the pecan business my father runs.  Currently, I am the Quality Assurance Tech, Social Media Marketer, Front End Junior Developer, and Blog Writer for ArvidOutdoors.com, a search engine allowing people to find guided outdoor experiences created & written by my husband, Steve Halvorson.

I have built my coding knowledge by participating in development conferences, workshops, & meet-ups and have completed countless lessons & tutorials from online resources.  I spend the majority of my time creating small Angular projects and have become highly skilled in research & problem solving (in other words, I am a proficient Googler).

Angular Girls conferences are one of my favorite things to take part in, and recently I was a mentor for the first time for ngConf 2021!   I am proficient with CSS, SASS, HTML, Typescript, Angular, Firebase, Stackblitz, Visual Studio Code, Git/GitHub/GitKraken, Weebly, Microsoft Office, Google Suite, and Adobe Creative Cloud. I have experience creating icons, logos, brochures, and business cards using Adobe Photoshop.

When I'm not coding, you can find me taking pictures of my kids, cat, & backyard birds, watching movies, and reading.  I’m usually doing all of this from my kitchen where I seem to constantly cook (and when I’m not cooking, I’m thinking about cooking).
