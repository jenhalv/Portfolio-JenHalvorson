About

Greetings! Thanks for checking out my portfolio.  My name is Jennifer Halvorson ~ I ventured into the programming world in 2019 after quitting my teaching job in the KC area to move to our family’s southwestern Missouri pecan farm.


I created this portfolio using Angular CLI and published Paul’s Pecans, a website dedicated to displaying information about pecan farming.  Currently, I am the Quality Assurance Tech, Social Media Marketer, Front End Junior Developer, and Blog Writer for ArvidOutdoors.com, a search engine allowing people to find guided outdoor experiences created & written by my husband, Steve Halvorson.  I have experience creating icons, logos, brochures, and business cards using Adobe Photoshop.


I have built my coding knowledge by participating in development conferences, workshops, & meet-ups and have completed countless lessons & tutorials from online resources.  I spend the majority of my time creating small Angular projects and have become highly skilled in research & problem solving (in other words, I am a proficient Googler).  I enjoy participating in Angular Girls conferences and was a mentor for the first time for ngConf 2021!  You can contact me at jenhalv06@gmail.com.
